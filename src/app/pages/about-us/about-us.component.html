Our Company Profile

Our company is called Sliver Lotus Courier Service (SLCS) Co., Ltd, and it was founded on October 18th, 2020, under Company Registration No. (128079254) for the purposes of Trading & Logistics businesses. This is a thriving business, and we have seen positive growth in less than two years. The company’s primary goal is to provide fast, secure and convenient logistics services.

SLCS offers international logistics services. The Company provides Air transportation, Sea transportation, Land transportation, Express Services, Warehousing, Customs declaration, and Other services.

Our company is expanding significantly, and we anticipate continuing to grow in the fiscal years 2022-2023. The company’s ultimate goal is to meet the demands of clients and customers by providing adequate, efficient and low-cost transportation and logistics services. Our employees and other associated staff are doing their best and working hard to deliver the service to meet client’s requirements. We will persevere to make this organization one of the top firms in the world of logistics. We ensure and accept the responsibility of supplying our consumers with high-quality services.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading logistics service provider in South East Asia and the world, offering safe and cost-effective logistics services.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a variety of services to meet the needs of our customers. In order to achieve long-term success and profitability, we are committed to integrity, reliability, longevity and productivity.

Our Strength

To ensure the organization’s proper functioning, 14 executives work in clearing, freight forwarding and transportation, all of whom are supervised by managers and executives. We have a mentor with 20-30 years of experience who guides us through every aspect of operations and gives us advice on how to build a profitable and successful company.
